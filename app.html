Who we serve, in order

Each stage only works once the one before it does

Renters and buyers first — the most frequent, most painful version of the trust gap. Landlords and agencies join the same ledger once that side is real, not before.

02 · Individual landlords

The same Trust Ledger, sold back to them — a verified landlord attracts serious tenants faster.

03 · Managing agencies

Qualified tenant leads once real search volume exists — we stay in trust and verification, not rent collection.
